MODERN CHINESE

Step into the world of Xia Fei Fei, an upscale Chinese restaurant that beckons with the finest flavours of Beijing, Sichuan, and Shanghainese cuisine. With an elegant and comfortable setting, Xia Fei Fei invites guests to sit back and savour the flavours of China.
